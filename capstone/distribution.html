Introduction

Though grasshoppers are generally restricted to certain habitats, we sometimes find accidentals, or grasshoppers outside their usual habitat. The number of accidentals in a given area also varies throughout the year. Researchers surveyed different sites alomng the elvation gradient outside of Boulder throughout the year to see where the accidentals were showing up. In this case, they were looking for plains grasshoppers in mountainous habitats.
