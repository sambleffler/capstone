Introduction

Grasshoppers are ectotherms, or cold-blooded animals. This menas they depend on their environment to regulate their body temperature. This means that there is a minimum temperature and a maximum temperature where they can survive, but they'll perform best at a certain optimal temperature. These temperature ranges vary from species to species. High temperature specialist species perfrom best at warm temperatures, low temperature specialist species perfom best at cool temperatures, while generlaist grasshoppers are adapted for a wide range of tempertures.
