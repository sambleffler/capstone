PHENOLOGY

Introduction

Phenology is the study of an organism's lifecylce and how it is affected by the environment. Grasshoppers have six life stages, with 1 being a nymph and 6 being an adult. We predict grasshopper growth using Growing Degree Days (GDD). Each speices of grasshopper has a minimum temperature where it will grow, and every degree Celsius above that temperature is counts as 1 GDD. When they accumulate enough GDDs, they will be adults.
